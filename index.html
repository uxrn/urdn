Use Cases

Digital Identity

Create censorship-resistant domains on the Handshake network. Your domain works as a universal, cross-chain identifier for accessing decentralized apps and platforms. You have full control over your online identity. Learn how to access your domain at HDNS.

Resistant Websites

Manage your domain's DNS records and enable HTTPS access via DANE or upload your website to IPFS for decentralized hosting. Avoid the bureaucracy of ICANN and build on the only naming protocol with a truly decentralized root zone.

Wallet Naming

Get a human readable name to replace all of your lengthy crypto addresses. Stop copying and pasting and risking typos. Use your HNS domain to easily send or receive any cryptocurrency, token, or NFT. Transact between domains easily and efficiently.
